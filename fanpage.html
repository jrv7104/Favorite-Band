Sevendust

Bio

Sevendust is a hard rock band that formed in Atlanta, Georgia in 1994. The band released its self-titled debut album in 1997. Since then Sevendust has released 13 studio albums, including its latest release Blood and Stone.

    Band Members
  1. Lajon Witherspoon (vocals)
  2. Vince Hornsby (bass, backing vocals)
  3. Morgan Rose (drums, backing vocals)
  4. Clint Lowery (guitar, backing vocals)
  5. John Connley (guitar, backing vocals)
    Former Members
  • Sonny Mayo (guitar, backing vocals)
